My name is Shriram Sadashivajois. I'm an Experimental Astrophysicist, a Marie-Sklodowska Curie fellow in Physics at Royal Holloway - University of London. I write under a nom de plume of Shriram Jois. During the PhD days, I was a member of ADMX; now with the DarkSide-20k collaboration. I live in the U.K. Before coming to the U.K., I did a PhD in Physics at the University of Florida. I wrote a dissertation titled, "A Search for Relic Axions and Their Frequency Modulation". For a Marie-Sklodowska Curie fellowship, I wrote a proposal to search Solar Axions in the Silicon of the Silicon Photo Multipliers (SiPM) through an Axio-Electric effect.
