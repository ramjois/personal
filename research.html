Dark Matter

Dark Mater makes up nearly 85% of our galaxy. Axions and Wimps are prominents candidates to probe. Axions were hypothesized to explain the invariance of the strong interactions under the transformations P and CP, while rest of the standard model violates it.

My professor Pierre Sikivie proposed an experiment to detect these axions using a microwave cavity. Under a strong inhomogenous magnetic field, the axion decay into a microwave photon. This is a Sikivie conversion.
